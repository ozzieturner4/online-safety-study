Questionnaire Instructions

This questionnaire is designed to test your understanding of the information covered in the learning chapters. You will be presented with 10 multiple-choice questions based on common online risks, including phishing, scams, and safe online behaviour. Some questions may include short scenarios to help you apply what you have learned. Please read each question carefully and select the answer you believe is correct. Once you have answered all the questions, click the "Submit" button at the bottom of the page. Your answers will be marked automatically, and your final score will be shown as a percentage. A score of 60% or higher is considered a pass. No personal data is collected during this questionnaire, and you may retake it as many times as you wish.
